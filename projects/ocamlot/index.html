Let the conquest begin!

Instructions

Hello, mighty ruler. You have been tasked with defending the empire of Ocamlot.

You are the blue team, and the enemy is the red team. Grey towers are neutral and can be overtaken.

To over take an enemy tower, click and drag from one your towers and release on another tower.

Different types of towers send different types of troops: foot soldiers and cavalry.

Each tower regenerates troops to its max capacity. Sending troops halfs the current capacity.

Skills

Lightning kills 15 troops of an opposing tower.

Freeze stuns an opposing tower for 10 seconds.

Regen permanantly triples troop regeneration rate of the tower.
